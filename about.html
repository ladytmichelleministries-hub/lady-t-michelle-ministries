Welcome

My name is Lady T. Michelle, better known as Latasha McFadgon, and I am the founder of Lady T. Michelle Ministries. I have been married for over 16 years to my husband, Pastor James McFadgon of Holy Ground Ministry of Love. As a wife and ministry leader, I carry a deep burden to see marriages strengthened, restored, and covered in God’s grace.

This ministry was birthed from a passion for helping singles and couples build relationships that honor God. I believe marriage is sacred, intentional, and worth fighting for—especially in a world where relationships are often challenged, rushed, or misunderstood.

Through prayer, lived experience, and biblical wisdom, I felt called to create faith-based resources that support you at every stage of your journey—whether you are preparing for marriage, nurturing connection, or walking through difficult seasons.

Our Marriage Journey

Like many couples, my husband and I have learned that marriage requires faith, humility, communication, and continual surrender to God. We’ve experienced seasons of growth, stretching, and restoration—and we’ve seen how prayer changes hearts and renews unity. Our journey has reinforced this truth: when God remains at the center, love can be rebuilt, peace can return, and purpose can be restored.

That is why the journals offered through this ministry are designed to help you slow down, reflect, communicate honestly, and intentionally invite God into every part of your relationship. Each page is created with purpose—to strengthen faith, deepen connection, and remind you that you are not alone.

“God is our refuge and strength, a very present help in trouble.” — Psalm 46:1
