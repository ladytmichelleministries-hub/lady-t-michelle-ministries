About Lady T. Michelle

Welcome

My name is Lady T. Michelle, better known as Latasha McFadgon, and I am the founder of Lady T. Michelle Ministries. I have been married for over 16 years to my husband, Pastor James McFadgon of Holy Ground Ministry of Love.

This ministry was birthed from a deep passion for strengthening marriages, restoring hope, and preparing hearts through faith. I believe marriage is sacred, intentional, and worth fighting for—especially in a world where relationships are often challenged, rushed, or misunderstood.

Through prayer, lived experience, and biblical wisdom, I felt called to create faith-based resources that support singles and couples at every stage of their journey—whether preparing for marriage, nurturing connection, or navigating difficult seasons.

Our Marriage Journey

Like many couples, my husband and I have learned that marriage requires faith, humility, communication, and continual surrender to God. We have experienced seasons of growth, stretching, and restoration—and we have witnessed how prayer can renew unity and restore purpose.

The journals offered through this ministry are designed to help couples and individuals slow down, reflect, communicate honestly, and intentionally invite God into every part of their relationship. Each page is created with purpose—to strengthen faith, deepen connection, and remind you that you are not alone.

“God is our refuge and strength, a very present help in trouble.” — Psalm 46:1
